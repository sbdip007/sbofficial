Best Mods of 2022

VAPORESSO TARGET 100

The VAPORESSO TARGET 100 is a stylish mod that takes a single 21700 battery—also compatible with 20700s as well as 18650s if used with an adapter. It features a water-resistant braid that makes it very comfortable to hold while providing some extra ruggedness, making it a great option for fans of the outdoors. The mod hits up to 100 watts and comes in five color options.

Geekvape Aegis M100

The sequel to the Aegis Mini is an IP68 rated mod that fires up to 100 watts and houses a 2500 mAh battery. It comes equipped with Geekvape's Stable Output Buck-boost technology and introduces a handy lock switch that makes it safer to carry. The M100 houses a large color screen and comes in six color options.

Innokin Coolfire Z80

The latest in the long line of Coolfire mods comes with a variety of innovative features and the classy design that's synonymous with the series. It is equipped with Innokin's FØ technology and Refresh mode, which combine to extend coil life and boost flavor. The mod is available standalone as well as in a kit with the Zenith II tank.
